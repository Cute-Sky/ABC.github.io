School of Law

The School of Law offers legal education and research, taught by distinguished faculty and provides practical experience opportunities for students.

School of Information Technology

The School of IT offers education and research in computer science, software engineering, cybersecurity, data science, and emerging technologies.

The School of Journalism offers education and research in journalism, media studies, communication, and related fields, preparing students for careers in journalism, broadcasting, public relations, and other media-related professions
